Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens after I submit the form? +
As soon as you submit, our team is notified immediately. A loan specialist will be assigned to review your application and contact you via call or SMS — usually within the hour during business hours.
How is my application reviewed? +
Every application at LoanHub Philippines is carefully reviewed by one of our trained loan specialists. We take a thorough approach — looking at your full situation to find the best loan option for your needs.
What are the basic requirements? +
You need a valid government-issued ID, proof of income (payslip, ITR, or bank statement), and an active contact number. Your loan specialist will guide you on any additional documents needed based on the loan type you're applying for.
How long does the approval process take? +
Our team typically responds within 1 hour of receiving your application. Full approval and fund release can happen within the same day for most loan types, depending on how quickly documents are submitted.
Is there a fee to apply? +
Absolutely none. Applying is completely free. All fees and charges will only be clearly explained to you by your loan specialist before you agree to anything — no surprises, no hidden costs.
How much can I borrow? +
Loan amounts vary by loan type. Personal and Professional Loans go up to ₱1M, Business Loans up to ₱3M, Home Loans up to ₱5M, Car Loans up to ₱2M, Medical and Sangla OR/CR Loans up to ₱500K, and OFW Loans up to ₱500K. Your specialist will help determine the right amount based on your income and capacity.
What loan types do you offer? +
We offer Home Loans, Car Loans, Business Loans, Medical Loans, Professional Loans, Sangla OR/CR, and OFW Loans. Each product is designed for a specific need — click on any loan card above to see the full requirements checklist for that product.
Can I apply if I'm self-employed or a freelancer? +
Yes! We welcome applications from self-employed individuals, freelancers, and online sellers. You'll need to provide bank statements for the last 6 months, proof of income (client contracts or remittance records), and a government-issued ID. Your specialist will work with you to find the best fit.
Can OFWs apply even if they're abroad? +
Yes! OFWs can apply through a family representative here in the Philippines using a Special Power of Attorney (SPA). The SPA must be notarized or consularized. Our specialists are experienced in handling OFW applications and will guide your representative every step of the way.
What is Sangla OR/CR and how does it work? +
Sangla OR/CR is a type of secured loan where you use your vehicle's Official Receipt (OR) and Certificate of Registration (CR) as collateral. Your vehicle must be fully paid, registered under your name, and not more than 10 years old. You keep your vehicle while the loan is active — you just surrender the OR/CR documents as security.
What are the repayment options? +
We offer flexible repayment terms depending on the loan type — ranging from 6 to 60 months. Payment can be scheduled weekly, semi-monthly, or monthly. Your specialist will work with your income cycle to set up a schedule that's comfortable and manageable for you.
Is my personal information safe? +
Absolutely. We take data privacy seriously and comply with the Data Privacy Act of the Philippines (RA 10173). Your personal information is only used for loan processing purposes and is never shared with third parties without your consent.
What if my application gets declined? +
If your application doesn't qualify for one loan product, our specialist will explore other available options that may be a better fit for your situation. We work with multiple lending partners and we will do our best to find a solution that works for you.
How do I reach your team? +
You can reach us via call, SMS, or Facebook Messenger. Once you submit an application, a specialist will proactively reach out to you. You can also visit our Facebook page directly at fb.com/LoanHubPhilippines for quick assistance.
Do you have partner lending institutions? +
Yes. LoanHub Philippines works with accredited lending partners including Asialink Finance Corporation, Cepat Kredit, Equicom Savings Bank, and CTBC Bank Philippines. Depending on your profile and loan type, your application may be processed through one of these institutions — your specialist will explain which partner is the best match for your needs.
