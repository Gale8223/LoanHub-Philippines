Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens after I submit the form? +
As soon as you submit, our team is notified immediately. A loan specialist will be assigned to review your application and contact you via call or SMS — usually within the hour during business hours.
How is my application reviewed? +
Every application at LoanHub Philippines is carefully reviewed by one of our trained loan specialists. We take a thorough approach — looking at your full situation to find the best loan option for your needs.
What are the basic requirements? +
You need a valid government-issued ID, proof of income (payslip, ITR, or bank statement), and an active contact number. Your loan specialist will guide you on any additional documents needed.
How long does the approval process take? +
Our team typically responds within 1 hour of receiving your application. Full approval and fund release can happen within the same day for most loan types, depending on how quickly documents are submitted.
Is there a fee to apply? +
Absolutely none. Applying is completely free. All fees and charges will only be clearly explained to you by your loan specialist before you agree to anything — no surprises, no hidden costs.
